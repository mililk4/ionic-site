Introducing the Ionic Framework

Ionic is the most advanced HTML5 mobile app development framework, optimized for building amazing cross-platform native apps. You've never seen HTML5 feel so native!

We built Ionic to bring HTML5 on mobile into the future. We wanted a serious framework that could be used to build the amazing, custom apps that the best app developers were making. A framework that transcended the URL bar and was best friends with AngularJS (while not being restricted to it).

We knew Ionic had to be 100% free and open source, so we are releasing it under a permissive MIT license that can be used in any project. We will be building and extending our current products to support Ionic, as well as building new services around it.

We are just putting the finishing touches on Ionic, and are shooting to release sometime in mid to late October 2013.
